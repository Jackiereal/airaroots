# Executive Summary

> Audience: Investors, new team members, AI assistants
> Purpose: Understand what Airaroots is and why it exists in 5 minutes

---

## What We Are Building

Airaroots is a **Hospitality Business Operating System** — not another property management tool.

The difference: traditional PMS tools track bookings. Airaroots runs the entire business. It answers the question every property owner actually has:

> "Is my property making money, and what should I do about it?"

---

## The Problem

Property owners managing 1–500 short-term rental properties today are forced to use 5–10 disconnected tools:

| Tool | Purpose | Problem |
|------|---------|---------|
| Airbnb dashboard | Reservations | Siloed, no financial clarity |
| Booking.com dashboard | More reservations | Different interface, different data |
| Excel / Google Sheets | Finance tracking | Manual, error-prone, no automation |
| WhatsApp / email | Guest communication | No history, no automation |
| Physical checklist | Housekeeping | Not trackable, not reportable |
| Separate accounting tool | P&L | Never accurate, always delayed |

The result: owners make decisions based on gut feel, not data. They overpay on expenses, underprice their properties, miss maintenance issues, and lose guests who never return.

---

## Our Solution

One platform that:

1. **Centralizes all reservations** from Airbnb, Booking.com, and direct channels into a single calendar
2. **Derives all finance** automatically from reservation data — no manual entry
3. **Orchestrates operations** by triggering housekeeping, maintenance, and inventory tasks from reservation events
4. **Communicates automatically** with guests via WhatsApp, email, and SMS at the right moment
5. **Surfaces AI insights** on pricing, occupancy, expenses, and property health
6. **Gives owners a portal** to see their property performance without calling their manager

## Business Model

SaaS subscription with three tiers:

| Tier | Target | Price |
|------|--------|-------|
| Starter | 1–5 properties | ₹2,999/month |
| Growth | 6–25 properties | ₹7,999/month |
| Pro | 26–100 properties | ₹19,999/month |
| Enterprise | 100+ properties | Custom |

Plus optional add-ons: AI Insights, White Label, Additional Users.

Revenue from channel manager is not commission-based — pure SaaS, which differentiates from Guesty, Hostaway, and Lodgify who take 1–3% of booking value.
