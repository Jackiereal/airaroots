# Product Positioning

---

## Positioning Statement

**For property management companies and independent property owners in India and Southeast Asia who manage short-term rentals, Airaroots is a hospitality operating system that eliminates the need for 5+ disconnected tools by automating reservations, finance, operations, and guest communication in one platform. Unlike Guesty, Hostaway, or Lodgify, Airaroots is built for the Indian market, charges a flat SaaS fee (no commission), is WhatsApp-first, and uses AI to proactively surface insights rather than just report history.**

## Our Differentiation

### 1. Flat SaaS, No Commission
Guesty, Hostaway, and others charge 1–3% per booking on top of subscription. We charge a flat monthly fee. For a property earning ₹3L/month, that's ₹6,000–₹9,000 saved per month per property vs. commission models.

### 2. India-First Design
- Razorpay integration (UPI, netbanking, credit card)
- INR-native finance
- WhatsApp-first guest communication (not email-first)
- Indian calendar events (Diwali, Holi, cricket season) as pricing signals
- Regional GST compliance

### 3. AI-Native
Competitors added AI as a marketing feature in 2024. We design AI as infrastructure from Phase 1. By Phase 9, AI copilot generates actionable recommendations, not just charts.

### 4. Operations-First
Most channel managers stop at reservations. We own the full workflow: reservation → housekeeping task → maintenance history → inventory → vendor payment. This creates deep workflow lock-in.

### 5. Owner Transparency
PMCs manage properties on behalf of owners. Owners currently get PDFs once a month. Airaroots gives owners a live portal: see their property's revenue, occupancy, expenses, and health score in real time.

## Positioning by Segment

### Individual Property Owner (1–5 properties)
Pain: Manages everything manually. Uses Airbnb dashboard + WhatsApp + Excel.
Message: "Airaroots replaces your spreadsheet, your WhatsApp reminders, and your manual calendar — all in one app."
Key feature: Starter tier, quick onboarding, Airbnb import.

### Boutique Brand / Operator (5–25 properties)
Pain: Needs team collaboration, multi-property view, consistent operations.
Message: "Manage your entire portfolio from one dashboard. Your team knows what to do and when."
Key features: Multi-property calendar, housekeeping assignment, team roles.

### Property Management Company (25–500 properties)
Pain: Scale without proportional headcount. Owner reporting. Channel conflicts.
Message: "Scale your portfolio without scaling your team. Your owners see their performance live."
Key features: Automation engine, owner portal, bulk operations, API access.
