# Product Vision

---

## Vision Statement

**Airaroots will be the operating system for every hospitality business in Asia — from a single villa to a 500-property management company — giving owners complete visibility, automated operations, and AI-driven decisions.**

---

## The World We Are Building Toward

In the Airaroots-enabled world:

A property owner wakes up and opens Airaroots on their phone. They see:
- Occupancy for the next 30 days across all 12 properties
- A pricing alert: "Unit 4 is priced 18% below market for the long weekend — raise to ₹8,400"
- A task notification: "Checkout at Unit 7 today — housekeeping assigned"
- A guest message that was automatically sent at 8am: "Welcome! Your check-in code is 4821"
- Net cash position: up ₹1.2L from last month

They make zero manual decisions before breakfast.

---

## Core Product Belief

**Reservations are the heartbeat of every hospitality business.**

Everything else — finance, operations, communication, analytics — is a downstream effect of reservations.

Design consequence: the reservation engine must be perfect before anything else is built. A corrupt or incomplete reservation record corrupts everything downstream.

---

## What Airaroots Is NOT

| Not This | Why |
|----------|-----|
| A booking engine (OTA) | We do not take guest bookings — we manage the property side |
| A channel manager only | Channel sync is one feature, not the whole product |
| An accounting tool | Finance is derived automatically, not manually entered |
| A spreadsheet replacement | We automate workflows, not just display data |
| A guest-facing app | We serve property managers and owners, not guests |

## Product Pillars

### Pillar 1: Universal Reservation Calendar
Single view of all reservations across all properties and all channels. Multi-property timeline. Drag-and-drop modifications. Conflict detection. Blocked dates.

### Pillar 2: Automated Finance
Every reservation automatically creates revenue records. Expenses tracked by category. P&L, cash flow, and valuation calculated in real time. No spreadsheet required.

### Pillar 3: Operations Orchestration
Reservations automatically trigger housekeeping tasks. Maintenance requests tracked with photos, vendor assignments, and resolution history. Inventory tracked per property.

### Pillar 4: Intelligent Communication
Right message, right channel, right time. WhatsApp-first. Templates with variables. Automated triggers: booking confirmation, pre-arrival, check-in instructions, mid-stay, checkout, review request.

### Pillar 5: Revenue Intelligence
Dynamic pricing recommendations. Occupancy forecasting. Expense anomaly detection. Market benchmarking. Owner-level financial reporting. Investor-grade reports.

### Pillar 6: AI Copilot
Natural language queries: "What was my best performing property last quarter?" AI surfaces anomalies before they become problems. Predictive cash flow. Pricing optimization.

---

## Design Philosophy

**1. Opinionated defaults, configurable exceptions**
80% of users should be able to onboard and run their first property in under 10 minutes without reading docs. The remaining 20% of power users get full configuration.

**2. Mobile-first after Phase 7**
Operations staff (housekeeping, maintenance) use phones. Owner dashboards must work on tablet. Admin users may use desktop.

**3. Data density over simplicity**
Our users are sophisticated operators. They want dense information displays, not dumbed-down cards. Think Bloomberg Terminal, not consumer app.

**4. Automation over notification**
If the system can do it automatically, it should. Notifications are the fallback. We never alert unless action is required.

**5. Never lose data**
Soft deletes only for financial records. Every mutation is audited. Undo is always possible for non-financial records.
