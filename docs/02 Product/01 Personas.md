# User Personas

---

## Persona 1: Rahul — The Individual Property Owner

**Profile:**
- Age: 38
- Location: Bengaluru (manages property in Coorg)
- Properties: 2 villas
- Channels: Airbnb + direct bookings via Instagram DMs
- Technical comfort: Medium (uses apps, not spreadsheets)
- Revenue: ₹8–12L/year per property

**Current workflow:**
1. Checks Airbnb app for new bookings
2. Manually WhatsApps guests check-in instructions
3. Calls housekeeper after each checkout
4. Maintains Excel sheet for expenses and revenue
5. Cries at tax season

**Pain points:**
- Double bookings when taking direct bookings during Airbnb stay
- Forgetting to send check-in instructions
- Never knows actual profitability until month-end
- Can't tell which months were good vs. bad historically

**Goals:**
- Know his net income in real time
- Automate WhatsApp messages
- Single calendar for all bookings
- Track which expenses are eating his margin

**Airaroots usage:** Starter plan. Uses calendar, finance, and basic automation.

**Success metric:** Spends <15 minutes/week on admin instead of 3–4 hours.

## Persona 2: Priya — The PMC Operations Manager

**Profile:**
- Age: 29
- Location: Goa
- Role: Operations Manager at a 40-property PMC
- Properties managed: 40 villas across North and South Goa
- Technical comfort: High
- Reports to: PMC Owner

**Current workflow:**
1. Checks 3 different Airbnb accounts + Booking.com
2. Coordinates housekeeping via WhatsApp group (40 members)
3. Assigns tasks manually from a Notion board
4. Sends owner reports manually via email at month-end
5. Manages 12 vendors via phone calls

**Pain points:**
- No single view of what's clean, what needs cleaning, what's occupied
- Owners call constantly asking about their property performance
- Maintenance issues fall through the cracks
- No accountability — can't prove tasks were completed
- Month-end reporting takes 2 full days

**Goals:**
- One screen that shows all 40 properties' status at a glance
- Automated housekeeping task assignment after checkout
- Photo-based task completion proof
- Owner portal so owners stop calling
- Automated month-end reports

**Airaroots usage:** Pro plan. Heaviest user. Uses every module. On mobile all day.

**Success metric:** Month-end reporting reduced from 2 days to 30 minutes.

## Persona 3: Vikram — The PMC Owner

**Profile:**
- Age: 47
- Location: Mumbai (PMC operations in multiple cities)
- Properties managed: 120+ across Goa, Lonavala, Alibaug
- Technical comfort: Low-medium (needs clean, simple dashboards)
- Focus: Revenue, growth, investor reporting

**Current workflow:**
1. Receives WhatsApp updates from operations managers
2. Reviews Excel sheets prepared by accountant
3. Makes pricing decisions based on gut feel
4. Cannot explain to investors which properties are underperforming
5. Relies entirely on trust in his team — no system visibility

**Pain points:**
- No visibility into real-time performance without asking someone
- Cannot compare properties against each other
- Does not know actual net income per property until accountant finishes
- Makes pricing decisions without market data
- Cannot generate investor-grade reports

**Goals:**
- Portfolio dashboard showing all properties' KPIs
- Alerts when something is wrong (low occupancy, high expense, pricing gap)
- AI-driven pricing recommendations
- One-click investor reports
- Net income per property, not just gross revenue

**Airaroots usage:** Pro/Enterprise plan. Primarily dashboard consumer. Uses mobile. Reviews AI insights weekly.

**Success metric:** Makes pricing decisions with data instead of gut feel.

## Persona 4: Ananya — The Property Owner (Investor)

**Profile:**
- Age: 52
- Location: Delhi
- Properties: 5 properties managed by a PMC
- Technical comfort: Low (only uses phone)
- Relationship to platform: Client portal user, not admin

**Current workflow:**
1. Calls her PMC manager every 2 weeks for updates
2. Receives a PDF report once a month
3. Has no real-time visibility into occupancy or revenue
4. Trusts the PMC entirely — no independent verification

**Pain points:**
- No visibility until month-end PDF
- Can't verify if occupancy rates match what PMC claims
- Doesn't know if her properties are competitively priced
- Can't compare her portfolio performance to previous years

**Goals:**
- Real-time dashboard for her 5 properties
- Revenue, occupancy, and expense visibility without calling PMC
- Annual performance summary for tax purposes
- Alerts for significant events (maintenance, long gap in bookings)

**Airaroots usage:** Owner portal (read-only). Mobile-only. Views weekly.

**Success metric:** Zero calls to PMC about performance questions.

## Persona 5: Deepak — The Housekeeper / Field Staff

**Profile:**
- Age: 25
- Location: Goa
- Role: Housekeeper for 8 properties
- Technical comfort: Low (basic WhatsApp user, smartphone owner)
- Language: Konkani / Hindi, limited English

**Current workflow:**
1. Gets WhatsApp message from manager: "Clean unit 5 today"
2. Heads to property, cleans it
3. Sends a WhatsApp message back: "Done"
4. No structured checklist, no photo verification

**Pain points:**
- Unclear what checklist to follow
- No way to report maintenance issues formally
- Gets blamed for missed items with no way to prove what was done
- Scheduling conflicts — sent to wrong property sometimes

**Goals:**
- Clear task list on phone
- Checklist of what to clean
- Photo upload for proof of completion
- Easy way to report a maintenance issue seen during cleaning

**Airaroots usage:** Mobile-only. Operations app. Very simple UI. Local language support (future).

**Success metric:** Zero disputes about task completion.
