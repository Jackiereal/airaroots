- 14 days, all Pro features enabled
- No credit card required to start
- Credit card required on Day 14 to continue

**Trial to Paid Conversion:**
- Email sequence: Days 3, 7, 10, 13

**Cancellation:**
- Cancel anytime from billing settings
- Access retained until end of billing period
- Data retained for 90 days post-cancellation
- CSV export available during retention period

## Pricing Rationale

**Why flat SaaS, not per-booking commission?**

A property earning ₹3L/month at 1% commission = ₹3,000/month just to the PMS.
At ₹7,999/month flat, we're cheaper for PMCs managing 3+ properties at that revenue level.
Commission models penalize growth — we reward it.

**Why not per-property pricing?**

Per-property pricing creates resistance to adding properties and creates an artificial ceiling on PMC growth. Flat tier pricing encourages them to add all their inventory.

**Why India pricing, not global pricing?**

Global competitors price in USD for a US business context. ₹7,999 is ~$96 — accessible for even small Indian PMCs. We win on price in India and can expand globally later with localized pricing.
